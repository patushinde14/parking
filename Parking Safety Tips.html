SAFETY TIPS

When utilizing a parking slot finder website, prioritize your safety by following these essential tips. Begin by opting for reputable and well-reviewed apps or websites that offer reliable parking information.
Always verify reservations and choose parking partners that prioritize security.

Be Aware of Surroundings

Stay alert to your surroundings; be aware of people and activities nearby.

Lock Your Vehicle

Always lock your vehicle, even if you'll only be away for a short time.

Plan Your Route

Plan your route in advance to avoid getting lost, especially in unfamiliar areas.

Stay Visible

Choose parking spots that are visible to others, reducing the risk of being targeted.

Know Emergency Exits

Be aware of emergency exits and pathways in parking garages or lots.

Trust Your Instincts

If a parking area feels unsafe, trust your instincts and find an alternative.
